Short Bio

I am a second year Ph.D. student at IMDEA Software Institute, in Madrid, Spain, under the supervision of Assistant Professor Thaleia Dimitra Doudali. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and computer systems. I am interested in the ways we can use machine learning methods inside computer systems software to improve their performance and resource efficiency. In 2022, I earned my Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens in Greece.
